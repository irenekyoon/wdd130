Whitewater Rafting

Whitewater Rafting is committed to providing safe, secure family-oriented experiences while at the same time introducing clients to the marvels and exhilaration of whitewater rafting. We strive to provide every client a once-in-a-lifetime experience whether it is their first time or their fiftieth time.

History

We are a multi-generational family runned company that began in 1961, when Davis Thompson fell in love with the area and decided to provide others with the same love that he felt for the Okangan River.
