Just a little background on the Manila, Cebu City and Urdaneta temples. Gordon B. Hinckley, then a member of the First Presidency of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints, dedicated the Manila Philippines Temple on 25 September 1984. Thomas S. Monson, president of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints, dedicated the Cebu City Philippines Temple June 13, 2010. President Dallin H. Oaks, then First Counselor in the First Presidency, presided at the dedication of the Urdaneta Philippines Temple on Sunday, April 28, 2024. Each temple is a holy House of the Lord. They are houses of prayer and worship. I have also found them to be places of peace, healing and joy. I testify that Jesus is the Living Christ, the Only Begotten Son of God. He is my Savior and Redeemer and yours too. Life is better with Jesus Christ and temple covenants.

Philippines

The Philippines is a country where the people are warm-hearted and kind, always ready to help and lift others up. There are 14 temples of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints. Four are currently operating, four are under construction, and six have been announced and are in the planning stage.
